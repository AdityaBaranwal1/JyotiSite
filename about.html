Who We Are

The Light of Life

Jeevan Jyoti — जीवन ज्योति — means “the light of life.” We are a community of seniors in Rockland County, New York, who gather every week to learn, laugh, stretch, paint, sing, and look after one another. Most of our programs meet on Zoom, so neighbors can join from their own kitchen table — and anyone who prefers the telephone is just as welcome.

Our weekly classes are led by volunteers and friends of the community: gentle yoga and guided meditation, watercolor painting, music hours, plain-spoken talks on health and finances, and patient help with computers and phones. Every program is free, and every program begins the same way — with someone glad you came.

Our Partners

With Thanks

Jeevan Jyoti works in partnership with the Rockland County Office for the Aging, whose support helps keep every class free and open to all seniors in the county. We are grateful, too, to the volunteer instructors who give their time each week.
